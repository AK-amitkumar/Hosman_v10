How to use

Patients

You can enter all the details of patients.All other tabs will show details aboth patients like , medicaments, lab test details, etc...


Patients Card Report

Reports for Patients Card can be generated.


Patients Medication History Report

Reports for Patients Medication History can be generated.


Patients Vaccinations History Report

Reports for Patients Vaccinations History can be generated.


Patients Diseases Report

Reports for Patients Diseases can be generated.


Patients Lab Test Report

Reports for Patients Lab Test can be generated.


Appointments

From this tab user can create an appointment for a patient with tiny details like physician , consultation service etc..


Here we have fearture showing calendar to show appointments on particular dates.


Families

This feature will help user to enter the family members and their contact details for future reference to contact them in case of emergency or in the time of need.


Prescriptions

Prescription is feature where after diagnosis of the patient , you can enter the details of prescripted medicaments, its dosage and other details


Prescription Report

Reports for Prescription can be generated.


Patient Hospitalization

This form will help administrative department to save the details of patient about his/her hospitalization. Patients bed details, hospitalization date & expected discharge date, and other details can be saved here.


Once confirmed patient registration registration will be in confirmed stage. From this stage it can be cancelled.


If patient gets admitted to hospital then you will see discharge patient button.


Pediatric Symptoms Checklist

this feature provides built in PSC tool in here. You just have to select the answer and save the answer given by the patients.


Newborn

Newborm form proived all the necessary criteria to fill about newborn.
Basic Details: Mother's name and other details.
APGAR SCore: pulses, respiration and other details.
Neonatal signs and symptoms, Neonatal Reflex, diseases and Other details.


Newborm Card Report

Reports for Newborm Card can be generated.


Lab Requests

Lab Requests can be created for patient with test type.


Today's Draft Lab Requests

Today's Draft Lab Requests can be seen directly.


Lab Tests Results

Lab Results data can be saved here once Lab tests are done.


Surgery

This is a list of surgeries with description of surgery, classification of surgery and other details.

Prescription Invoice.
